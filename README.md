# CS-250-Sprint-Review-and-Retrospective
# How do I interpret user needs and implement them into a program? How does creating “user stories” help with this?
First I would gather pertinent information through conducting interviews with users to get information about the product. I would then create the user stories by identifying a goal and benefit. Finally I would order the stories into the product backlog in order of importance to decide what will be worked on during the next sprint. 

# How do I approach developing programs? What Agile processes do I hope to incorporate into my future development work?
The Agile processes I hope to incorporate into future development is the entirety of the Scrum framework. The Scrum framework promotes team collaboration, transparency, accountability and flexibility. I personally think the best part of Scrum is its flexibility, Scrum is designed to handle constant changes that may arise when developing software which allows the team to constantly adjust to users needs. 

# What does it mean to be a good team member in software development?
Being a good team member in software development means to ensure I do my part and keep myself accountable for my work. A good team member listens to other team members and takes in feedback to ensure improvement. I also need to make sure I give feedback back to my fellow team members to ensure they improve as well.
